### [1] [FreeAnimate: Training-Free Human Image Animation with Preview-Guided Denoising](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.06885)
*Yuan Zeng,Yujia Shi,Zongqing Lu,QingMin Liao*

Main category: cs.CV

TL;DR: FreeAnimate 是一个无需训练的人体图像动画框架，利用扩散模型的内在能力实现时间一致性、身份保持和背景稳定，通过预览生成策略和注意力模块，效果优于现有方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有基于扩散模型的人体图像动画方法需要大量训练数据和资源，限制了泛化能力和可访问性。本文旨在提出一种无需训练的框架来解决这一问题。

Method: 提出 FreeAnimate 框架：(1) 预览生成策略，从生成的预览帧中提供时间和结构先验，引导姿态对齐和背景一致性；(2) Inversion-Boosted Attention 模块保证时间一致性；(3) Reference-Anchored Self-Attention 模块保证身份保持。

Result: FreeAnimate 在无需训练的情况下，优于现有无训练竞争对手和基于训练的基线方法，生成质量与最先进方法相当，并在多种数据集上展现出强泛化能力。

Conclusion: FreeAnimate 成功证明了无需额外训练即可利用扩散模型实现高质量人体图像动画，为提升方法的泛化性和可访问性提供了新思路。

Abstract: Human Image Animation has seen significant advancements, primarily driven by diffusion models. However, existing methods typically demand substantial training data and resources to achieve high-quality results, limiting generalization and accessibility. In this work, we introduce \emph{FreeAnimate}, a training-free framework that leverages the inherent capabilities of image diffusion models to enable temporal consistency, identity preservation, and background stability. Our approach incorporates a novel preview generation strategy that provides temporal and structural priors from generated preview frames, effectively guiding pose alignment and background consistency without training. Additionally, FreeAnimate introduces Inversion-Boosted Attention and Reference-Anchored Self-Attention modules to guarantee temporal consistency and identity preservation. Experimental results demonstrate that FreeAnimate outperforms existing training-free competitors and training-based baseline methods, achieving generation quality comparable to state-of-the-art methods and offering robust generalization across diverse datasets. Our project page is at https://freeani.github.io/.

### [2] [Detecting Temporally Localized Manipulations in Authentic Video Streams](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07090)
*Okan Umur,Ali Emre Güşlü,Ibrahim Delibasoglu*

Main category: cs.CV

TL;DR: 本文指出现有视频伪造检测数据集无法很好地模拟"在真实视频中插入一段短时间、高逼真度的篡改片段"这一场景。为此，作者回顾并分析了现有数据集局限性，构建了一个专门针对此类局部篡改检测的新数据集，并基于DINOv3特征评估了两种检测方法（线性探针和连续帧相似性法），建立了初步基准。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有数据集主要关注全局伪造（如整脸替换、物体移除），缺乏对"真实视频中插入一段短而逼真的篡改片段，原始视频继续播放"这一现实场景的建模能力。

Method: 1) 分析并指出现有数据集在时间局部篡改场景上的不足；2) 构建专门针对短时高保真篡改片段的新测试集；3) 评估两种方法：基于DINOv3特征的线性探针（三种阈值策略）和基于连续帧相似性的时间边界检测方法。

Result: 两种方法在自定义测试集上建立了初步基准；实验表明需要内容自适应的阈值机制才能有效检测局部篡改片段。

Conclusion: 现有数据集在时间局部篡改检测上存在空白，本文提出的新数据集和基准实验展示了该场景的挑战性，强调了内容自适应阈值的重要性。

Abstract: The rapid advancement of video editing and generative artificial intelligence technologies has made realistic video manipulation increasingly accessible. Although existing datasets have significantly advanced research in deepfake detection, object removal, and video inpainting, they do not adequately model scenarios in which a short manipulated segment is inserted into an otherwise authentic video and the original video continues afterward. In this study, we review representative datasets from the literature, analyze their characteristics, and discuss their limitations with respect to temporally localized realistic manipulation detection. Based on this analysis, we motivate the need for a new dataset specifically designed for authentic videos containing short and highly realistic manipulated intervals. Finally, we evaluate two complementary approaches on our custom-curated test set to establish an initial benchmark for this challenging scenario. The first employs a linear probe on DINOv3 features, assessed under three thresholding strategies. The second leverages DINOv3 features with a consecutive frame similarity-based method to detect temporal manipulation boundaries. Together, these experiments provide an initial benchmark for partially manipulated video detection and highlight the need for content-adaptive thresholding mechanisms. The dataset, code, and supplementary materials are publicly available at https://github.com/OkanUmur/temporally-localized-video-manipulation-detection.

### [3] [CULTURESCORE: Evaluating Cultural Faithfulness in Video Generation Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07311)
*Anku Rani,Wei Dai,Shravan Nayak,Pattie Maes,Mahdi M. Kalayeh,Paul Pu Liang*

Main category: cs.CV

TL;DR: 本文提出CultureScore，一个从身份、背景、行为三个维度评估视频生成模型文化忠诚度的框架。在10个国家、3个模型、6180个视频的评估中，最佳模型仅达到56.8%的文化得分，行为维度最低（<52%）。人类偏好与CultureScore一致，但与视觉质量评分（VideoScore）呈反比，表明文化忠诚度是公平视频生成的关键标准。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有视频生成评估指标（如VideoScore）仅关注视觉质量，无法衡量模型对全球多元文化的准确呈现。一个将合十礼错误生成为握手的模型与正确生成的模型获得相同分数，这暴露出文化忠诚度评估的缺失。

Method: 提出CultureScore组合评估框架，将文化忠诚度分解为三个维度：（1）身份——谁被呈现；（2）背景——文化本地化的场景；（3）行为——规范的姿势和互动。在10个国家使用3个SOTA模型生成6180个视频进行系统评估。

Result: 最佳模型整体CultureScore仅56.8%，行为维度成为最难达标的维度（所有模型均低于52%）。人类偏好排名与CultureScore方向一致，但与VideoScore呈反向关系：视觉质量最高的模型在文化维度上排名最低。

Conclusion: 当前视频生成模型无法实现文化忠诚的生成，该能力是公平视频生成不可或缺的标准。CultureScore填补了文化忠实度评估的空白，为模型改进提供细粒度指导。

Abstract: As video generation models like Veo 3.1 and LTX-2 advance, their ability to accurately represent diverse global cultures remains a critical yet understudied frontier. Current metrics, such as VideoScore, only measure visual quality but offer no mechanism for assessing cultural faithfulness. Consequently, a model that replaces a Namaste with a handshake receives the same score as one that generates the gesture correctly. We propose CultureScore, a compositional evaluation framework that decomposes cultural faithfulness into three granular dimensions: Identity (who is represented), Context (culturally localized background), and Behavior (normative gestures and interactions). We operationalize this framework through an evaluation suite spanning 10 countries, yielding 6,180 generated videos across three state-of-the-art models. Our evaluation reveals that no current model achieves culturally faithful video generation: the best-performing model reaches only 56.8\% overall CultureScore, with Behavior the most challenging dimension, which remains below 52\% across all models. Furthermore, human preference rankings align directionally with CultureScore but are inverted relative to VideoScore; the highest-scoring model on visual quality was ranked last by annotators, underscoring that cultural faithfulness is an essential criterion for equitable video generation.

### [4] [The Lipreading Gap: Do VSR Models Perceive Visual Speech Like Human Lipreaders?](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07435)
*Rishabh Jain,Naomi Harte*

Main category: cs.CV

TL;DR: 本文对比了三种视觉语音识别（VSR）模型与人类基线在MaFI数据集上的表现，发现尽管VSR模型总体准确率更高，但其成功与失败的单词模式与人类不同，模型主要依赖训练数据中的语言线索而非视觉感知能力。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探究当前超越人类水平的VSR模型是否真的具备了类似于人类的视觉语音感知能力。

Method: 在MaFI单词级唇读数据集上，使用词、字符、音素和视位度量，比较三种VSR系统与人类基线，并加入文本n-gram基线对照。

Result: VSR模型总体准确率更高，但错误模式与人类不同；模型错误更多由训练词频解释而非视觉信息量；模型在最难的视位上提升最大，对视觉清晰度的依赖远弱于人类。

Conclusion: VSR系统主要依赖训练数据中的语言线索而非视觉感知，未能将视觉特征绑定为有意义的词语，不具备人类式的视觉语音感知能力。

Abstract: Visual speech recognition (VSR) models now surpass human lipreaders on benchmarks, but do such gains establish human-like visual speech perception? To explore this, we compare three VSR systems with human baselines on the MaFI word-level lipreading dataset using word, character, phoneme, and viseme-level metrics. Although models achieve higher overall accuracy, they succeed and fail on different words than humans. A text-only n-gram baseline given only a few initial phonemes rivals human lipreading. VSR word-level errors are consistently better explained by training word frequency than by the visual informativeness of words. Viseme accuracies, confusion matrices and human-model correlations further show that models gain most on visemes humans find hardest, and show much weaker dependence on visual clarity. Our work demonstrates that VSR systems rely primarily on language cues from training data rather than visual perception, failing to bind visual features into meaningful words.

### [5] [Streaming Video Generation with Streaming Force Control](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.07508)
*Hanhui Wang,Yiming Xie,Haiwen Feng,Zhaoyang Lv,Shenlong Wang,Huaizu Jiang*

Main category: cs.CV

TL;DR: StreamForce是一个流式视频生成框架，通过连续力输入实现物理可控的视频生成，支持局部和全局时变力，因果统一模型，在单GPU上可达16.6 FPS，在力一致性和运动真实性上达到SOTA。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有视频模型存在三个主要问题：1) 不同力类型需要训练不同模型；2) 假设力是固定的而非时变的；3) 依赖非因果处理。因此需要构建一个统一的、因果的、能实时响应时变力的视频生成框架。

Method: 1) 设计统一力表示作为控制信号；2) 开发蒸馏流程实现力可控视频生成；3) 结合自回归效率与力响应能力，保持光度和动态真实性。

Result: 单GPU上运行速度达16.6 FPS，在力一致性和运动真实性上达到当前最优水平。

Conclusion: StreamForce成功实现了因果统一的力可控流式视频生成框架，能够实时响应局部和全局时变力，兼顾效率与物理真实感。

Abstract: We introduce StreamForce, a streaming video generation framework that enables physically grounded control through continuous force inputs. Unlike prior video models that train separate models for different force types, assume fixed forces, or rely on non-causal processing, StreamForce is a causal and unified model that responds instantly and coherently to both local and global, time-varying forces. To achieve this, we design a unified force representation as a control signal and develop a distillation pipeline for force-controllable video generation. Our model combines autoregressive efficiency with force responsiveness, sustaining stable photometric and dynamic realism. StreamForce runs at up to 16.6 FPS on a single GPU, achieving state-of-the-art performance in both force adherence and motion realism. Project website: https://neu-vi.github.io/StreamForce/

</details>
